About Us

JBG Cleaning Service began in 2017 when a group of teenage friends set out to make a difference. From a local effort, we grew into a trusted family business by 2019. Now with eight years’ experience, we deliver exceptional cleaning across London—built on hard work, care, and excellence.

Our journey started when few teens in East London saw how cleanliness impacted health—especially in schools where dust allergies and hygiene were major concerns. What began as a small solution for our community has since become a London-wide small family business creating valuable opportunities for young people to earn while making spaces safer and cleaner.

Why Choose Us?

When you hire JBG, you’re not just getting a spotless space—you’re supporting a business that puts people and purpose first. Let us bring that same dedication to your home or workplace.

Our Services

At JBG Cleaning Services, we adapt to your needs—whether you require a single deep clean, emergency call-out, or regular maintenance on a daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly basis. For businesses and long-term clients, we also offer flexible contract arrangements. Every job, big or small, receives the same meticulous attention to detail, because your space deserves nothing less.

We keep your home consistently fresh with meticulous attention to detail. Our regular maintenance cleans ensure comfort and hygien.

  • Customised home cleaning
